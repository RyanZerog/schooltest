Artificial Intelligence Research

Pushing the boundaries of machine intelligence

Research Focus Areas

Our AI research program explores cutting-edge technologies and methodologies to develop intelligent systems capable of solving complex real-world problems. We focus on several key areas:

Deep Learning & Neural Networks

Developing advanced neural network architectures for computer vision, natural language processing, and multimodal learning. Our research explores optimization techniques, transfer learning, and interpretability of deep learning models.

深度学习和神经网络：开发用于计算机视觉、自然语言处理和多模态学习的先进神经网络架构。我们的研究探索优化技术、迁移学习和深度学习模型的可解释性。

Reinforcement Learning & Robotics

Investigating reinforcement learning algorithms for robotic control, autonomous navigation, and human-robot interaction. We are particularly interested in sample-efficient learning and safe exploration methods.

强化学习和机器人技术：研究用于机器人控制、自主导航和人机交互的强化学习算法。我们特别关注样本高效学习和安全探索方法。

AI in Healthcare

Applying AI technologies to medical imaging analysis, disease prediction, personalized treatment, and drug discovery. We collaborate closely with medical professionals to ensure our solutions address critical healthcare challenges.

医疗保健中的人工智能：将人工智能技术应用于医学图像分析、疾病预测、个性化治疗和药物发现。我们与医疗专业人员密切合作，确保我们的解决方案能够应对关键的医疗保健挑战。
