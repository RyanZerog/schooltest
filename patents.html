2023

Method and System for Rapid Detection of Viral Pathogens Using Nanopore Technology

Inventors: Zhang Li, Johnson Sarah, Chen Michael Patent No: CN123456789A Filed: March 15, 2023

A novel method utilizing nanopore technology for rapid and accurate detection of viral pathogens in biological samples. The system integrates machine learning algorithms to identify viral signatures in real-time, enabling early diagnosis and intervention in infectious disease management.

一种利用纳米孔技术在生物样本中快速准确检测病毒病原体的新方法。该系统集成了机器学习算法，可实时识别病毒特征，实现传染病管理中的早期诊断和干预。

Nanopore Technology Viral Detection Machine Learning Diagnostic Methods

Quantum Neural Network Architecture for Enhanced Machine Learning Processing

Inventors: Wang David, Smith John, Kumar Rahul Patent No: CN987654321B Filed: January 27, 2023

An advanced quantum computing architecture that integrates quantum principles with neural network design to significantly improve processing capabilities for complex machine learning tasks. The invention enables exponential speedup in training deep learning models while reducing energy consumption.

一种将量子原理与神经网络设计相结合的先进量子计算架构，显著提高了复杂机器学习任务的处理能力。该发明实现了深度学习模型训练的指数级加速，同时降低了能源消耗。

Quantum Computing Neural Networks Deep Learning Energy Efficient Computing

2022

Perovskite-Silicon Tandem Solar Cell with Enhanced Conversion Efficiency

Inventors: Liu Mei, Garcia Antonio, Patel Anika Patent No: CN246813579C Filed: November 8, 2022

A novel tandem solar cell design combining perovskite and silicon technologies to achieve significantly higher photovoltaic conversion efficiency. The layered structure optimizes light absorption across multiple wavelengths and includes innovative interface materials to minimize energy losses.

一种新型叠层太阳能电池设计，结合钙钛矿和硅技术，实现显著更高的光伏转换效率。该分层结构优化了多波长的光吸收，并包含创新的界面材料以最小化能量损失。

Solar Energy Perovskite Photovoltaics Renewable Energy

Biodegradable Smart Polymer with Controlled Drug Release Properties

Inventors: Chen Li, Rodriguez Elena, Williams Thomas Patent No: CN135792468D Filed: August 5, 2022

A biodegradable polymer system that responds to specific physiological conditions to control drug release rates. The material incorporates environmentally friendly components and provides programmable degradation timelines, making it ideal for targeted therapeutic delivery applications.

一种响应特定生理条件以控制药物释放速率的生物可降解聚合物系统。该材料包含环保成分，并提供可编程的降解时间线，非常适合靶向治疗递送应用。

Biodegradable Polymers Drug Delivery Smart Materials Medical Applications

2021

CRISPR-Based Method for Targeted Genetic Modification in Agricultural Crops

Inventors: Zhang Wei, Johnson Robert, Gupta Priya Patent No: CN864297531E Filed: October 12, 2021

An advanced gene editing technique utilizing CRISPR-Cas systems for precise genetic modifications in agricultural crops. The method includes proprietary delivery mechanisms and editing protocols that enhance success rates while minimizing off-target effects, specifically designed for improving crop yields and resistance to environmental stressors.

一种利用CRISPR-Cas系统对农作物进行精确基因修饰的先进基因编辑技术。该方法包括专有的递送机制和编辑协议，在提高成功率的同时最小化脱靶效应，专门设计用于提高作物产量和对环境胁迫的抗性。

CRISPR Gene Editing Agriculture Food Security

Patent Application Process

专利申请流程

For Researchers

If you are a researcher at our institute and have developed an innovation that may be patentable, please follow these steps:

  1. Complete an Invention Disclosure Form
  2. Submit to the Intellectual Property Office
  3. Participate in the innovation assessment meeting
  4. Work with our patent attorneys during the application process

For more information, please contact our IP Office at ip@academicresearch.edu.

如果您是我们研究所的研究人员，并且开发了可能获得专利的创新成果，请按照以下步骤操作：

  1. 填写发明披露表
  2. 提交给知识产权办公室
  3. 参与创新评估会议
  4. 在申请过程中与我们的专利律师合作
For Industry Partners

We welcome opportunities to license our patented technologies or collaborate on further development. Our partnerships include:

  • Technology licensing agreements
  • Joint development projects
  • Industry-sponsored research
  • Startups and spinoff companies

To explore partnership opportunities, please contact our Technology Transfer Office at tech-transfer@academicresearch.edu.

我们欢迎授权我们的专利技术或合作进一步开发的机会。我们的合作包括：

  • 技术许可协议
  • 联合开发项目
